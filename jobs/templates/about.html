About CareerConnect

Connecting talented individuals with their dream careers and helping companies find the perfect candidates.

Our Mission

CareerConnect is dedicated to bridging the gap between job seekers and employers. We believe that finding the right job or the right candidate should be simple, efficient, and accessible to everyone. Our platform is designed to make the job search and recruitment process as smooth as possible.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every talented individual finds meaningful work that aligns with their skills and aspirations, and every company has access to a diverse pool of qualified candidates. Through technology and innovation, we're making this vision a reality.

About This Project

CareerConnect is a comprehensive job portal web application developed as a 6th semester academic project. The platform demonstrates modern web development practices using Django, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Features: User authentication, role-based dashboards (Job Seeker & Employer), job posting and application management, advanced search and filtering, profile management, and responsive design.
